About MR.COFFEE

We believe every cup tells a story. MR.COFFEE is more than just a coffee shop—it's a community built on passion, quality, and connection. Discover our journey and what makes us special.

Susanto Liman

CEO & Founder
At Mr.Coffe, our dream is to serve high quality coffee, made with the freshest local ingredients to customers across Indonesia and the rest of the world.
I started this journey in a small 3x3 meter stall, with nothing but a secondhand coffee machine and an unwavering passion. Every morning, I woke up early not just to brew coffee, but to brew hope—that each cup I served could be the reason someone smiles. From the first aroma to the last sip, MR.COFFEE was born out of love for the process, simplicity, and human connection. Now, I want to bring that warmth to the digital world, so everyone can experience the story behind each cup—no matter where they are.

Our Mission

To make all taste coffee every day, spreading warmth and connection through every cup.

Our Vision

To be the most beloved coffee community, inspiring creativity and connection worldwide.

Company Story

In the heart of a bustling city, MR.COFFEE began as a small, classic coffee shop with quirky charm and the aroma of freshly brewed wonders. Our shop, with mismatched chairs and walls covered in customer doodles, became a cozy haven for dreamers, creatives, and anyone in need of inspiration. It wasn't just about coffee, it was about connection, laughter, and the warm smiles that filled the air. Today, we bring that same spirit to the digital world, inviting everyone to share in the magic of MR.COFFEE.
